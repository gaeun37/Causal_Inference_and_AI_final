197 feet long. Now, this pipe had a
sharp turn in it, a circumstance that
rendered useless the usual methods
adopted to get the wires through.
First the men endeavored to blow a
tape line through the pipe, but to no
avail. Then they tried to force the tape
through water. This, too, was a failure
by reason of the sharp bend in the
pipe line.
After a long squabble some one suggested
that a mouse be employed to carry
the string through the pipe. Accordingly
after some difficulty a mouse was
captured and put to work. A thread
was tied to the little creature’s leg. It
was placed in the mouth of the pipe and
given a decided impetus with a whiff of
compressed air.
It was not more than a minute or so
before the rodent appeared at the other,
end of the pipe with the thread. The
rest of the work was easy. The thxead
drew the tape through the pipe and the

Natives of Hawaiian Islands Dive in

This is drift that is brought down from

Heating in orthodox fashion, it prompt- j
ly sinks to the bottom. As the freshet j
gains headway, down come the heavy

less bloeks or split into kindling.
